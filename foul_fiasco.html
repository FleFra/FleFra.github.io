Foul Fiasco

A 2D runner game.

About Foul Fiasco

"Foul Fiasco" is a 2D game developed in Unity for a school project where we had to create an arcade game with a team of 5 people. For this project, each team had different restrictions. Our team had to make a game for kids under 12 in greyscale, with the theme 'Sports'. We decided to make a game where you're running away from the referee of a football game, while simultaneously dodging obstacles and scoring goals for extra points. The game has simple mechanics and an online leaderboard.

Features:

  • Try to get the high score!
  • Retro pixel graphics!
  • Arcade-like controls!
  • Addicting gameplay!

My Contribution

During the development of foul fiasco, I worked on the following key-features:

  • Pixel art: I created most of the art in foul fiasco. It was quite difficult to make a greyscale game, since the risk of the game looking washed out was very prominent. I chose to make the background very light so the character and obstacles would 'pop' more.
  • Animations: Made sure all the animations played at the right time and worked properly in the game.
  • Programming: Mainly focused on random map generation and movement.

This project was very fun, our team worked well and we we're proud of the game we made in such a short timeframe.

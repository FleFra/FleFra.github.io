About Me

Hi, I’m Fleur — a 21-year-old software development student with a passion for cars, video games, and all things creative tech. I focus mainly on game development, especially using C# and Unity to build interactive, immersive experiences. I love the process of bringing ideas to life through code — whether it’s prototyping gameplay or polishing small visual details that make a world feel real. Outside of coding, I’m into car culture. I love working on my own car, going for drives, and just spending time around machines. I also enjoy exploring art and design, from dabbling in pixel art to learning 3D modeling in Blender. I’m always curious, always experimenting, and always looking for new ways to express creativity through tech. When I’m not behind a screen or under the hood, you’ll probably find me out on the road — camera in hand, dog by my side.

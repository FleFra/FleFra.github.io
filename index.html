About Me

Hi, I’m Fleur Spannenberg, a 20-year-old software development student from Enschede. My main focus is game development, and I primarily program in C# using Unity to create immersive and engaging projects. I also love exploring the artistic side of development. Whether it’s crafting intricate pixel art or experimenting with 3D modeling and rendering in Blender, I’m always eager to push my creative boundaries. While I’m not a Blender pro yet, I’m constantly practicing and learning new techniques. I’m also always excited to pick up a new programming language or creative tool to expand my skill set. Beyond programming and art, I spend my free time driving and working on my car, often with my camera in hand to capture anything that catches my eye. My dog is often by my side, whether we’re out on a drive, exploring nature, or enjoying a walk together.

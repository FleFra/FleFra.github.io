About Zomboolean

"Zomboolean" is a mobile game developed in Unity. It's supposed to be a simple mobile game you can pick up and put down easily without losing your progress. The game keeps track of many of the player's stats and the player can equip different weapons to suit their play style.

Features:

  • Expand your land!
  • Buy new weapons!
  • Upgrade your character!
  • Become as rich as possible!

My Contribution

I played an important role in the development of "Zomboolean," our exciting mobile zombie game. My contributions included:

  • Scrum Master: Managed the team's workflow by organizing sprints, conducting stand-ups, and ensuring the project stayed on track to meet deadlines.
  • 3D Modeling: Designed and developed several 3D models for in-game assets.
  • Team Collaboration: Coordinated tasks across the team, facilitating communication and resolving challenges during the development process.
  • Programming: Mainly focused on creating a save data system to save the players progress.
  • Bug fixing: Focused a lot on fixing several game-breaking bugs.

This project was a valuable opportunity to improve my leadership skills, and practise conflict resolving. There was a lot of tension in the team due to conflicts in between team members and team members not showing up. It was quite a challenge to get everyone to work together and get their work finished in time.
